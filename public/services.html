Salt is a service designed for businesses and customers alike to set up the scheduling and delivery of products. We set out to solve the problem of companies losing profit due to scheduling faults (double booking, unorganized scheduling, etc..). This service also allows the customer to select the products they want to be delivered at the time and location of their choosing. We believe you should never have a long window of time that your delivery might show up in. Instead, you should be able to free up your schedule and precious time and have confidence that what you need will be there when you need it.
